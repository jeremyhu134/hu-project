Secure family group chat software with end-to-end encryption.

Features

  • Data is kept within the family, and out of big tech companies' hands
  • Encryption - Keep trade secrets or business ideas strictly confidential.
  • User-friendly interface, yet can be modified as needed while also gaining front-end/back-end skills. This website is just a starting point, expect it to change.

How It Works

  1. Browse to the website and enter your secure credentials to access the chat network. (Future plans for desktop app and Android app.)
  2. Start Messaging, either direct messsages or different group discussion topic threads.
  3. Browse the "GOALS" page to see a list of current Objectives by the family, ranked in order of priority.
    • Goals can be long term or short term.
    • Goals can consist of large objectives, or short tasks, such as "buy XYZ real estate to rent out", to "do research on the real estate market surrounding Lehigh Valley".
    • There is a ticket system for goals such that when a goal is marked as completed, it is moved to the archive folder.
    • Goals possibly to be implemented with SQL, and giving each goal it's own private key.
